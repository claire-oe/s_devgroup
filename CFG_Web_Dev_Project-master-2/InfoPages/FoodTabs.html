Food: Facts

A plant-based diet is well-proven as the best for the environment (and your health!). Hover over the facts to find out more.

"Not eating dairy and meat is the biggest way to reduce your impact on the earth"
"More than 80% of farmland is used for livestock but it produces 18% of food calories and 37% of protein"
"Without meet and dairy consumption, global farmland use could be reduced by more than 75%"
"Foods associated with improved health often have low environmental impacts"
"Beef emits 192 times more carbon dioxide than nuts, per gram of protein it provides"
Considering both health and the environment, evidence suggests that eggs and poultry could be the least harmful animal products.
